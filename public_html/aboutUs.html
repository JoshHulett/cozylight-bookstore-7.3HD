Our Story

Discover the story behind CozyLight

Our First Chapter

CozyLight began as a corner bookstore tucked away on a quiet street, founded by two lifelong friends who shared a passion for stories that bring people together. What started with a small shelf of handpicked paperbacks soon blossomed into a welcoming space where readers of all ages could slow down, sip tea, and share their love of books.

Our Mission Statement

We believe stories have the power to comfort, connect, and inspire. CozyLight is dedicated to curating a cozy, inclusive literary experience—online and in-store—where every reader can find a little light in the pages they turn.

Our Values

At CozyLight, we value warmth, inclusivity, creativity, and authenticity. We support small authors, celebrate diverse voices, and believe bookstores should feel like home. Every title we carry and every event we host is chosen with heart.

Our Community

The heart of CozyLight isn't just the books—it's the people. From our book club regulars and local writers to first-time visitors, we've grown a vibrant community that thrives on kindness and shared curiosity. Together, we've created more than a shop; we've built a story-loving family.

A Plot Twist

Like any good story, ours had a twist: when the pandemic changed the world, we had to close our physical doors. But rather than fade, we adapted—launching our online store, virtual events, and blog. What could've been the end of our story became a new beginning.

The Next Chapter

Today, CozyLight continues to grow with new titles, heartfelt events, and an expanding digital presence. We're working on launching exclusive CozyLight editions, expanding our blog features, and opening a new flagship location. The story isn't over—it's just getting to the good part.
