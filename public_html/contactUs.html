CozyLight Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 1, 2025

At CozyLight Books, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you visit our website

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, billing and shipping address, and payment details when you place an order.
  • Account Information: Login credentials if you create an account with us.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, device type, and browser data, collected through cookies and analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Process and fulfill orders.
  • Send you order updates and promotional content (only if you opt-in).
  • Improve our website, products, and user experience.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure security.

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your information. We may share your data with:

  • Trusted third parties such as payment processors and shipping partners, strictly for order fulfillment.
  • Legal authorities, if required by law.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to remember your preferences, enhance your experience, and analyze website traffic. You can adjust cookie settings in your browser at any time.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or correct your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your information.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. While no online system is 100% secure, we do our best to safeguard your information.

7. Children's Privacy

Our site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under this age.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. If we make material changes, we will post a notice on our site.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: service.cozylight@anemail.com
